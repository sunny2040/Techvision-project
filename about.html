SAHYOG CARE: OUR FOUNDER'S INSPIRING JOURNEY

"In a world full of challenges, genuine change begins with a dream and a compassionate heart." These words perfectly encapsulate Shekhar Mahajan's journey and the birth of Sahyog Care for You in 2002. Shekhar, the General Secretary of Sahyog Care for You, possesses a varied professional background, holding post-graduate degrees in MSW and law. Additionally, he is an active philanthropist and a dedicated social reformer. With a firm commitment to creating positive and significant transformations in society, Shekhar has undertaken a mission to bring about constructive and meaningful change. Sahyog Care, his brainchild, swiftly carved a niche in both the social sector and the sustainability industry. Shekhar Mahajan's unwavering determination, coupled with his tender-hearted nature, fueled his aspirations for a better world. Leveraging his education, experience, and exceptional social skills, he channelled his passion into the creation of Sahyog Care, overcoming numerous challenges. Together with the Sahyog team, he exemplified the values of care, diligence, and empathy while strategizing and implementing transformative programs across nine states in India. Shekhar's true mettle shines through his ability to convert obstacles into opportunities. Through extensive research, he provided simple yet strategic solutions to communities in need, ensuring the availability of essential resources such as education, personal hygiene, sanitation, water conservation, and sustainable livelihoods. His profound understanding of the social sector, coupled with his innovative thinking, propelled Sahyog Care's growth over the years. This organization, borne out of its ingenuity and commitment, has established itself as a prominent entity within the realms of both social development and sustainability.

MISSION AND VISSION

At Sahyog Care, our mission is to uplift marginalized groups, empower individuals, and embody the principles of Sarvodaya, fostering universal upliftment in society. We envision a world where every child, woman, and elderly person is nurtured, protected, and empowered to lead a life of dignity, opportunity, and fulfillment.

OUR APPROACH

Assess

Assess Assessing and analyzing their current scenario, Sahyog reaches out to the community by using various tools, such as focus group discussions, meetings, campaigns, pilot studies etc.

Assist

Assist Assisting the community after assessment in the required field by building the capacity of the community through providing life skill trainings, or finding the prospective donors etc.

Access

Access Access the resources, Sahyog takes the further step synergizing with the community, to the extent that the community is equipped enough on its own. These could be in any shape or form be it be either cash, or kind or engaging the community in viable livelihood options.
