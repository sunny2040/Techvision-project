The word "Sahyog" means cooperation in Hindi. Working in cooperation with various stakeholders to bring about a difference in the lives of underprivileged, deprived women, children, their families, and communities is what Sahyog is all about. Sahyog Care is a registered voluntary NGO that has been working for the empowerment of marginalized sections of society since 2002. Sahyog Care was started with a view to bring smiles to the lives of such people.
